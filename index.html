JobDock

Cleaning business software — quote, schedule, and get paid in one app.

JobDock is scheduling and invoicing software for residential and commercial cleaning businesses. Owners and their crews create quotes, schedule and manage recurring jobs, take online bookings, track time and before/after job photos, send invoices, collect payments by card or ACH, and optionally sync their appointments to Google Calendar — all from one web and mobile app.
