# Contacts (CRM)

**Route:** Contacts in the sidebar → `/app/crm`

## Purpose

Stores customers and leads. Contacts attach to Quotes, Invoices, and Jobs.

## Typical workflow

1. Open **Contacts**.
2. Add or select a contact.
3. Use contact details to start a Quote, Invoice, or link a Job (from other areas of the app as available).

## Notification preferences

Contacts can have notification preferences used when sending quotes or invoices (for example **email**, **SMS**, or **both**). If a client is not receiving messages, confirm the contact’s email/phone and preference in the contact record, then check **Settings → Email Templates** and company contact fields.

## Troubleshooting

- **Missing contact on a document**: Open the quote or invoice and confirm the correct contact is selected.
- **Wrong person got the message**: Verify the contact’s email and phone and re-send from the quote or invoice screen after correcting the contact.
